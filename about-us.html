Mission Statement

Go Green Bay is a professional, woman-owned cleaning service company located local in Walnut Creek area. We bring high quality cleaning services to residential clients and offices. We respect our customers and meet their needs by providing quick and trustworthy services. Unlike traditional cleaning service companies that use toxic products, at Go Green Bay we care to cater a green home with our non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning products that are 100% effective and safe for children, pets and the environment. We strive to provide the best cleaning service to meet your satisfaction. If you want a superb and natural cleaning experience, book Now with Go Green Bay LLC Today!

Our Story

My name is Lucy Ivanov, I’m a wife and a mother of one lovely toddler boy. I started Go Green Bay in 2019 right after I graduated university. Before Go Green Bay was established, I was full time working and studying while taking care of my toddler. As a new mom, I struggled to maintain my home tidy on top of my busy schedule. I searched for a cleaning service near my area, but I failed to find a service that is both reliable and eco-friendly for my toddler. Once I graduated, I dedicated my time to creating Go Green Bay, and help other households with busy working parents and provide eco-friendly cleaning service that is safe for children and the environment. I believe everyone deserves a clean and comfortable home regardless of what life brings to you. We value your time, so you can entrust your home to us and go make precious memories with your loved ones.
